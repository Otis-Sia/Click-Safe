Security Tools & Templates

Practical tools and templates for implementing security measures

Secure Password Generator

Generate strong, random passwords for your accounts. Never reuse passwords across different sites!

Click "Generate" to create a password

Tip: A good password should be at least 16 characters long and include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Never use the same password on multiple sites. Consider using a password manager to store your passwords securely.

Security Policy Template

A comprehensive template to create your organization's cybersecurity policy. Customize it to fit your business needs.

What's Included:

  • Password requirements and management guidelines
  • Acceptable use policy for company resources
  • Data classification and handling procedures
  • Incident reporting and response protocols
  • Remote work security guidelines
  • BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) policy
  • Email and internet usage guidelines
  • Physical security requirements

Risk Assessment Template

Identify and evaluate potential cybersecurity risks to your organization.

Interactive Risk Assessment Questionnaire

Answer these questions to get a basic risk assessment:

1. Do you use the same password across multiple accounts?

2. Is two-factor authentication enabled on critical accounts?

3. Do you have regular data backups?

4. Are all your software and systems regularly updated?

5. Do employees receive regular security training?

Incident Response Plan Template

A step-by-step guide for responding to cybersecurity incidents.

Plan Components:

  • Preparation: Contact lists, communication channels, equipment needs
  • Detection & Analysis: How to identify and assess incidents
  • Containment: Steps to limit damage and prevent spread
  • Eradication: Removing threats from your systems
  • Recovery: Restoring systems and normal operations
  • Post-Incident: Review, documentation, and lessons learned
