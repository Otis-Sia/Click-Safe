Protect your personal information on social platforms

Understanding Social Media Privacy

Social media platforms collect vast amounts of personal data. Understanding privacy settings and safe sharing practices helps protect your identity and personal information.

Privacy Settings to Review

  • Profile visibility: Choose who can see your profile and posts
  • Friend/follow requests: Control who can connect with you
  • Location sharing: Disable location tags on posts
  • Search visibility: Manage whether you appear in search results
  • Tagging permissions: Approve tags before they appear on your profile
  • App permissions: Review and revoke third-party app access

What NOT to Share

  • Full birth date (birth year especially)
  • Home address or exact location
  • Phone numbers
  • Financial information
  • Real-time location updates
  • Vacation plans (wait until you return)
  • Photos of tickets, boarding passes, or IDs
  • Information that could answer security questions

Safe Sharing Practices

  • Think before you post: Once online, content can be permanent
  • Limit personal details: Keep sensitive information private
  • Review photo backgrounds: Check for sensitive information before posting
  • Use privacy groups: Share personal updates with close friends only
  • Be cautious with check-ins: Wait until you've left a location

Platform-Specific Tips

  • Facebook: Use "Friends" or custom lists instead of "Public"
  • Instagram: Switch to a private account, carefully vet followers
  • Twitter/X: Review who can reply to your tweets
  • LinkedIn: Control who can see your connections and activity
  • TikTok: Set account to private, disable duets and stitches from strangers

Protecting Against Social Engineering

  • Be wary of friend requests from people you don't know
  • Verify requests from existing friends (could be impersonation)
  • Don't click suspicious links, even from friends
  • Be cautious about quizzes and games that request profile access
  • Question messages asking for personal information or money
