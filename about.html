Making Security Simple

We believe everyone deserves to feel safe online.

Who We Are

SafeByte is a cybersecurity education initiative developed by the University of Nairobi Kili Rovers Crew, affiliated with the Kenya Scouts Association. As Rovers committed to community service and leadership, we recognized the growing need for accessible cybersecurity education in our communities. This project bridges the gap between complex technical security concepts and practical, everyday digital safety"”empowering individuals, families, and small businesses to navigate the digital world with confidence.

Our Mission

To serve our community by empowering individuals, families, and organizations with practical cybersecurity knowledge. Through education and service, we aim to create a safer digital environment for all Kenyans.

Our Values

Guided by Scout principles of service, integrity, and leadership, we provide verified, tested guidance in clear language. No technical jargon or fear tactics"”just practical, actionable cybersecurity education rooted in community service.
