Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2, 2026

SafeByte values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

We may collect personal information when you voluntarily provide it through our contact forms, newsletter subscriptions, or event registrations:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • School or organization affiliation (if provided)
  • Messages or form submissions
  • Phone number (only if you choose to provide it)

b) Non-Personal Information

We automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • Website usage statistics (pages visited, time spent, anonymized)
  • IP address (anonymized for analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data exclusively to:

  • Provide cybersecurity education and awareness materials
  • Respond to inquiries and feedback

  • Comply with legal obligations

We never sell or rent personal data. Your information is used solely for the purposes outlined above.

3. Data Protection & Security

We take data security seriously and implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to:
